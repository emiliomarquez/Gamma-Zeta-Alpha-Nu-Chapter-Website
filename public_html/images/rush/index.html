What is Gamma Zeta Alpha

Gamma Zeta Alpha Fraternity Inc. is the first Latino interest fraternity established on the west coast at California State University Chico on 1987. From its establishment Gamma Zeta Alpha Fraternity Inc. has been committed to Academic Excellence, Community Service and the maintenance of the Latino culture through brotherhood.

Gamma Zeta Alpha Fraternity Inc. Nu Chapter was established at the University of California San Diego on 2004 by 13 gentlemen. Since 2004 we have crossed 87 brothers into the brotherhood of Gamma Zeta Alpha Fraternity Inc. molding each of our brothers into future leaders through professionalism.

What is Rush

Rush is typically a 2-3 week period where you get to know the brothers of Gamma Zeta Alpha Fraternity Inc. and get a taste of what our fraternity is about. We host various events on and off campus and all events are completely free of charge with rides also provided as well.

Why Rush Gamma Zeta Alpha

Academics: One thing that we stress as Gammas is our commitment to our academics. That is why we hold every brother to the expectation of having a 2.5 GPA and higher. To accomplish this we have mandatory study hours that we must meet every week in order to have exceptional grades.

Community Service: Along with excelling in academics we make time to give back to our surrounding communities. Throughout the academic year we host various community service events such as toy drives, can drives, bone marrow drives etc.

Brotherhood: Whether it be playing sports, working out, studying, getting something to eat our brother are always hanging out. Throughout the academic year we host various brotherhood events as well where we get to know our alumni better and simply have fun. College only lasts four years but our brotherhood lasts for life and we truly mean that.

Networking: Not only can you network with the brother at UCSD but you can also network with over 1,000 brothers nationwide that can help you land your ideal career and can provide helpful tips to become a successful individual. Being a part of this brotherhood already gets your foot inside the door of the professional world.
