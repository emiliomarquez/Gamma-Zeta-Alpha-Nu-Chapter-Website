Gamma Zeta Alpha Fraternity, Inc. of UCSD Scholarship

Purpose

Gamma Zeta Alpha strongly believes in the importance of Academic Success. The purpose of the Gamma Zeta Alpha Fraternity, Inc. of UCSD Scholarship is to reward well-rounded Latinx scholars.

We are looking for individuals who reflect our 3 principles: Academic Excellence, Comminty Service, and Maintence of the Latino Culture through Brotherhood.

To be considered for the Gamma Zeta Alpha Fraternity, Inc. of UCSD Scholarship the applicant must meet the following requirements.

  • 3.5 minimum unweighted GPA.
  • Must be of Latinx descent.
  • Will be attending University of California, San Diego.
  • Will be a Full time student at UCSD (minimum 12 units).
  • Community service hours (if applicable).
  • Letter of recommendation “optional”.

Common FAQs

Do I have to be Latinx to apply?

We encourage all Latinx students to apply. However, we are primarily looking for individuals who have been impacted by the Latinx Culture. If you were influenced or raised around the Latinx Culture definitely apply.

Do I have to join the Fraternity when I attend UC San Diego?

No. The purpose of the Scholarship is not meant to force students to apply to our fraternity.

What is the reward and are there preliminary prizes?

The amount of the reward is an average of $500. The money depends on the amount of money we have raised throughout the year. Preliminary prizes are not promised, but have happened before.

I am currently attending UC San Diego, can I apply?

The scholarship is meant for first year freshmen because we want to insure that incoming students have a stable experience in hopes that they stay in school.

When will the scholarship be given?

Scholarship will be given Fall 2019 at UC San Diego. The winner will be notified through email in early September.
